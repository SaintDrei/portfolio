ABOUT ME

Programming is just like writing poetry, but in a beautiful, highly-organized language.

Andrei Mishael D. Santos is an artist/developer currently studying Bachelor of Science in Information Systems in De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde. He is also a practitioner of mixed martial arts. He is currently into Arnis, Wushu, and Karate. In addition, he is a gamer, spending most of his time playing First Person Shooter games such as Paladins, Heroes & Generals, and Unturned. When you look at it overall, Andrei’s a pretty well-rounded person with an interesting variety of hobbies. Prior to selecting Information Systems, he was torn on which degree program to select. Being interested in multiple disciplines, it was tough to choose just one. His main choices were Film, Arts, Literature, Medicine, and Information Technology. Andrei ended up in the Information Systems degree program because his father is an Information Security officer and because it's the more lucrative choice. He could fund his passions in art by having a lucrative job while his Information Systems degree could still serve as his pre-med degree. Today, having studied under the Information Systems degree program for three years, he has become a full-fledged developer. The way he develops is mobile-first, prioritizing how his website will be viewed on a mobile phone before adjusting it for the desktop view. He prefers to start from scratch and he makes use of containers to shape his website before filling them with data. He doesn't focus on his programming skills only though, he still maintains his other passions. He's currently hoping to win the Palanca Award, specifically in the Short Story and Script Writing categories.
